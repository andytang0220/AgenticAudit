# Vendor Code of Conduct & Supplier Standards

**Northwind Trading Co.**

## 1. Purpose & Scope

This document sets the minimum standards that every supplier, vendor, contractor, and third-party service provider ("Vendor") must meet to do business with Northwind Trading Co. ("Northwind"). It applies to all Vendors regardless of size, location, or contract value. Acceptance of a purchase order or execution of a contract with Northwind constitutes agreement to these standards.

## 2. Code Acknowledgment

2.1. Every Vendor must sign and return this Code of Conduct **within 30 calendar days** of contract execution or first purchase order, whichever is earlier.

2.2. The signature must be from an authorized officer of the Vendor with authority to bind the organization.

2.3. Northwind will not release payment until a signed acknowledgment is on file.

## 3. Insurance Requirements

Vendors must maintain, at their own expense, at minimum the following coverage and provide a Certificate of Insurance naming Northwind Trading Co. as an additional insured:

3.1. **Commercial General Liability:** not less than **$2,000,000 per occurrence** and **$4,000,000 in the aggregate**.

3.2. **Workers' Compensation:** as required by applicable law in the Vendor's jurisdiction, with Employer's Liability of not less than $1,000,000.

3.3. **Cyber/Data Liability:** Vendors that store, process, or transmit Northwind or Northwind-customer data must carry cyber liability coverage of not less than **$5,000,000 per claim**.

3.4. **Professional Liability (E&O):** Vendors providing professional or advisory services must carry not less than **$1,000,000 per claim**.

3.5. Vendors must provide at least **30 days' written notice** before any cancellation or material reduction in coverage.

## 4. Labor & Human Rights

4.1. **No child labor.** Vendors shall not employ any person under the age of 16, or under the local legal minimum working age if higher.

4.2. **No forced labor.** No use of forced, bonded, indentured, or involuntary prison labor. Workers must be free to leave employment with reasonable notice.

4.3. **Working hours.** Regular working hours shall not exceed 48 hours per week, and total hours including overtime shall not exceed **60 hours per week** except in genuine emergencies.

4.4. **Wages.** Vendors shall pay at least the legally mandated minimum wage and provide all legally required benefits.

4.5. **Non-discrimination.** Vendors shall not discriminate in hiring or employment based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, or any protected characteristic.

## 5. Anti-Corruption & Ethics

5.1. Vendors shall comply with the U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA), the UK Bribery Act, and all applicable anti-corruption laws.

5.2. Vendors shall not offer, promise, or give any bribe, kickback, or improper payment to any party in connection with Northwind business.

5.3. **Gifts & hospitality.** Vendors shall not provide gifts, meals, or entertainment exceeding **$100 in value** to any Northwind employee. Cash or cash-equivalent gifts of any amount are prohibited.

5.4. Vendors shall disclose any actual or potential conflict of interest, including any Northwind employee or their immediate family holding a financial interest in the Vendor.

## 6. Subcontracting

6.1. A Vendor may not subcontract more than **25% of the contracted scope of work** without Northwind's prior **written approval**.

6.2. Vendors remain fully responsible for the performance and compliance of any approved subcontractor, who must meet all standards in this Code.

## 7. Confidentiality & Data

7.1. Vendors shall keep all Northwind confidential information secret and use it only for the purpose of performing the contract.

7.2. Vendors handling Northwind or customer data must additionally comply with the Northwind Information Security & Data Protection Policy (NW-POL-SEC-002).

## 8. Environmental & Sourcing Standards

8.1. Vendors shall comply with all applicable environmental laws and maintain required permits.

8.2. **Conflict minerals.** Vendors supplying products containing tin, tantalum, tungsten, or gold shall, on request, declare the source and provide reasonable supply-chain due diligence.

## 9. Audit & Compliance

9.1. Northwind reserves the right to audit a Vendor's compliance with this Code, on **at least 10 business days' written notice**, no more than once per twelve-month period absent cause.

9.2. Vendors shall maintain records demonstrating compliance for a minimum of **three (3) years**.

9.3. Material non-compliance may result in corrective action plans, suspension, or termination of the relationship.

## 10. Payment Terms

10.1. Unless otherwise agreed in writing, Northwind's standard payment terms are **Net 45** from receipt of a valid, undisputed invoice.

10.2. Invoices must reference a valid Northwind purchase order number to be eligible for payment.
